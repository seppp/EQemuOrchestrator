# The Ultimate Solo-Server Bootstrapper Guide (Level 1-60)

When playing on your own EQEmu server without a player economy or Bazaar, you have to be self-sufficient. You cannot buy gear or spells from other players, so your progression path must deliberately intercept specific camps, vendors, and drops.

Here is the exact, step-by-step master plan to level your 18-man PBAoE team from 1 to 60, fully optimized for a solo-server botting environment, including gearing, spells, and skill management.

---

## 🛠️ Pre-Requisite: Skill Management (Preventing the "Fizzle Wipe")
On a solo server, you cannot rely on being over-geared to survive mistakes. If a spell fizzles (fails to cast) because your skill is too low, you wipe.
1. **Level 1 (Guildmaster):** Put 1 point into `Evocation` (Wiz, Mag, Enc), `Alteration` (Clr, Enc, Dru), and `Conjuration` (Mag). For the SK, put 1 point into `1H Slashing`, `Defense`, and `Taunt`.
2. **Every 5 Levels (The Training Camp):** Before moving to a new zone, sit your Enchanters in a safe spot. Have them chain-cast their lowest level stun (*Color Flux*) until they get the message that their `Evocation` skill is maxed for their level (`Skill Max = Level * 5 + 5`). Do the same for Wizards (cast a low-level PBAoE) and Clerics (cast a minor heal to max `Alteration`). 

*Specific Class Focuses:*
*   **Enchanters (Evocation & Alteration):** PBAoE Stuns are `Evocation` spells. Mez and Tash are `Alteration`. You must keep Evocation maxed at every level. 
*   **Wizards / Magicians (Evocation):** All PBAoE damage spells are `Evocation`. Max this immediately.
*   **Clerics (Alteration):** Complete Heal and all standard heals are `Alteration`. 
*   **Shadowknight (Defense & Taunt):** Your SK must take hits to level `Defense`, `Dodge`, `Parry`, and `Riposte`. RGMercs will auto-skill `Taunt`. Ensure your SK trains `Channeling` so they can cast instant Lifetaps without being interrupted by the 50 mobs hitting their back.

## 📖 The Critical Spell Shopping List
Buying every spell for 18 characters will bankrupt you. Only buy the critical spells for the AE engine. You can farm the plat for these just by vendoring the loot you get while AEing.

### Shadowknight (The Puller)
*   *Lvl 9:* **Clinging Darkness** (Instant-cast snare for splitting pulls)
*   *Lvl 15:* **Disease Cloud** (Fastest, cheapest aggro spell)
*   *Lvl 15+* **Lifetap Line** (Lifespike, Lifedraw, etc. - essential for self-healing while running)
*   *Lvl 30:* **Feign Death** (The single most important spell you will buy)

### Enchanters (The Stunners)
*   *Lvl 4, 16, 30, 52:* **Color Flux, Color Shift, Color Skew, Color Slant** (The PBAoE Stun rotation).
*   *Lvl 14+* **Breeze / Clarity / KEI** (Mana regeneration buffs).
*   *Lvl 4+* **Tashanian Line** (Lowers magic resist of the mobs so stuns don't get resisted).

### Wizards & Magicians (The Executioners)
*   *Wizards (Lvl 12, 16, 24, 34, 46, 51):* **Frostreaver, Fire Spiral, Cast Force, Circle of Force, Supernova, Jyll's**. You ONLY need these PBAoE damage spells. Skip everything else early on.
*   *Magicians (Lvl 24, 55):* **Modulating Rod** (Mana batteries) and **Call of the Hero** (Lvl 55).

### Clerics & Druids (Sustain)
*   *Cleric (Lvl 39):* **Complete Heal**. This is the only heal you will use from Lvl 39 to 60 on the SK.
*   *Druid (Lvl 14+):* **Damage Shield Line** (Thorns, Spikecoat) for Phase 1 Power Leveling.

## 🟢 Phase 1: Creation and the Noob Yard (Levels 1 - 20)

**The Setup:** Create all characters in **Freeport** or **Qeynos**. 
**The Strategy:** Do not AE yet. Just `/assist` the Shadowknight and have all 17 characters single-target nuke/melee whatever the SK targets.

*   **Zone (Lvl 1-5):** North Ro, East Freeport, or Qeynos Hills.
*   **Zone (Lvl 5-12):** **Kurn's Tower** (Kunark). Excellent XP modifier.
*   **Zone (Lvl 12-20):** **Paludal Caverns** (Luclin). Camp the Bandit or Fiend caves. The XP modifier here is absurdly high. You can start practicing small AE pulls here.
*   **The Gear Goal:** Pure AC for the Shadowknight. Do not farm gear here. Loot vendor trash (weapons, silks, pelts) and sell them until you have about 100 Platinum. Find a player (or use an offline trader) to craft a full suit of **Banded Armor** for your Shadowknight. Casters wear whatever random cloth pieces drop.

---

## 🟡 Phase 2: The Stun Engine Awakens (Levels 20 - 35)

**The Strategy:** You now have the tools to do small AE pulls. The SK grabs 5-10 mobs, brings them back, the Enchanters stun, and the Wizards PBAoE. 
**Crucial Check:** Did you max `Evocation` before doing this? If not, do it now.

*   **Zone (Lvl 20-25):** Highkeep (Goblin Basement). The goblins path beautifully and respawn fast.
*   **Zone (Lvl 25-35):** **Lower Guk** (Undead Side).
*   **Clicky to Camp (Level 20ish):** 
    *   **Journeyman's Boots (JBoots):** You are on a solo server. Your SK *needs* run speed to pull trains safely. Farm the *Ancient Cyclops* in the Ocean of Tears or South Ro to get the ring for the JBoots quest.
*   **The Gear Goal (Lower Guk):** Upgrading the SK's survivability and securing Haste.
    *   **Flowing Black Silk Sash** (Frenzied Ghoul) - 21% Haste for the SK.
    *   **Dark Mail Gauntlets & Shin Greaves** (Ghoul Cavalier) - Great AC for the SK.
    *   **Runed Cowl** / **Shining Metallic Robes** (Ghoul Arch Magus) - Excellent early caster/cleric gear.

---

## 🟠 Phase 3: The Mid-Game Meat Grinder (Levels 35 - 45)

**The Strategy:** At level 30, Enchanters get their 3rd stun. The stun-lock is now infinite. You can safely pull 20-30 mobs.

*   **Zone:** **City of Mist** (Courtyard) and **Karnor's Castle** (Entrance/Moat).
*   **The Gear Goal:** Massive XP and transitioning to Kunark gear.
    *   **Blood Ember Armor** (Random drops in Karnor's Castle). This is the Shadowknight Kunark class armor. Loot any pieces that drop and slap them on the SK.
    *   **Jarsath Scale Armor** (Random drops in Karnor's). Good filler melee gear.
    *   **Harmonic Dagger / Lute of the Howler** - Pick these up for your Bards if they drop.

---

## 🔴 Phase 4: The Golden Era (Levels 45 - 55)

**The Strategy:** You are now a fully operational wrecking ball. The SK pulls 40+ mobs at a time. The Cleric times a Complete Heal to land right as the SK stops moving. 

*   **Zone:** **Old Sebilis** (Disco/Crypt) and **Chardok** (Bridge/Royals).
*   **Clicky to Camp (Level 50):**
    *   **Worker Sledgemallet:** Farm faction in The Overthere. This is a clicky teleport to The Overthere, essential for moving the SK around Kunark quickly.
*   **The Gear Goal:** The best pre-Velious gear in the game.
    *   **What to get (Sebilis):**
        *   **Hierophant's Cloak** (Hierophant Prime) - One of the best items in the game for Clerics and SKs.
        *   **Sebilite Scale Armor** (Various) - High AC upgrades for the SK.
        *   **Froglok Bonecaster's Robe** (Tolapumj) - Huge mana boost for Casters.
    *   **What to get (Chardok):**
        *   **Insignia Protector** or **Sarnak Battle Shield** - Massive AC shields for the SK.
        *   **Lamentation** or **Green Jade Broadsword** - Excellent weapons for the SK.

## 🟣 Phase 5: The Luclin Endgame & Gear Farm (Levels 55 - 60)

**The Strategy:** To push the final levels to 60, you need massive Luclin mob density. Once you hit 60, you pause XP farming and transition purely into the **Velious Armor Farm** to gear up the entire squad for raid progression.

*   **Zone (XP):** **The Deep**. The "Shrieker Bridge" is a massive bridge filled entirely with Shriekers. Pulling the whole bridge yields insane XP.
*   **Zone (Gear):** **Kael Drakkel** (The Plate House / Arena).
*   **The Gear Goal (Thurgadin Armor):** Fully gearing *all 18 characters* in high-tier armor in a single weekend.
    *   Take the 18 characters to Kael Drakkel. AE pull the Frost Giants.
    *   The giants drop faction and Armor Gems (e.g., *Crushed Coral*, *Flawless Diamond*).
    *   More importantly, they drop **Corroded Plate** (For SK/Clr/Brd), **Torn Enchanted Silk** (For Wiz/Enc/Mag/Nec).
    *   Take these pieces and gems to **Thurgadin (Icewell Keep)** and turn them in for class-specific Thurgadin Quest Armor. 
    *   *Result:* Because you are a solo server, this is the only way to get full sets of high-tier armor for 18 characters without spending years killing single named mobs. Thurgadin armor will provide massive AC for the SK, and massive Mana/HP/Resists for the casters, fully preparing you for Luclin Raid content (Emperor Ssraeshza, Vex Thal).
